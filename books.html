"Johnny and Joshua Coming to Korea" is the first of a series of children's picture books that explores many cultures from the eyes of a young boy and his pet bird. The story celebrates the similarities between the Ghanaian and South Korean cultures. Joshua is happy living in Ghana with his father and pet bird, Johnny. One day Joshua's father informs him they must move from Ghana to South Korea but can't bring his best friend, Johnny.
