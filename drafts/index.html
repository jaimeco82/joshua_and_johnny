    • Johnny and Joshua, Coming to Korea is a new children’s picture book that celebrates the similarities between the Ghanaian and Korean cultures. One day Joshua’s father informs him they must move from Ghana to South Korea but can’t bring his best friend, Johnny, the bird.
    This book encompasses friendship, love, culture, family and the importance of diversity within all cultures. It is a perfect read to increase cultural awareness, diversity and understanding for students of all age groups. The book also includes discussion questions for educators and parents to facilitate dialogue.
    Joshua and Johnny Coming to Korea is the first of a book series that begins the journey of the family’s travels around the world. Each book shows the family visiting a new cou00while learning the new country’s language, food and culture.
    Join Joshua and his best friend, Johnny, as they explore the world and learn how to be brave.
