A Message from the Author


Hello, I'm EJ Asare. I'm Haitian-American and lived in South Korea for six years. My family loves to travel and explore new countries. This book series is based on a true story. My son's best friend is Johnny, an African parrot. Johnny and Joshua are inseparable. They do everything together, including traveling around the world and learning about new cultures, people, and languages. This series will travel to many different countries and explore what we all have in common while highlighting a culture's uniqueness. If you want the characters to visit your wonderful country, please send an email to me and explain why your country is AMAZING. I hope you enjoy exploring the world with Joshua and Johnny. Remember to be brave!
